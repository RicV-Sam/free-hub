How to use competitions ending this week

Why ending-soon competitions matter

Competition entry forms often close at a fixed date and time, and some promoters remove the form as soon as the deadline passes. Checking closing-soon listings first helps you avoid missing current competitions while they are still open.

This page keeps the focus on published, non-expired listings. For the full active index, browse the wider competitions hub.

If you want a narrower starting point, compare free competitions, purchase required competitions, win-a-car competitions, or category pages for cars, cash, vouchers, holidays and tech.

What to check before entering

  • Confirm the closing date, closing time and timezone on the official promoter page.
  • Check eligibility, age limits, regional limits, purchase rules and how winners are contacted.
  • Use the official promoter link from each Freehub listing instead of unofficial social comments or copied forms.
  • Use the competition closing date checklist when a deadline is close.

Understanding entry labels

  • Free entry: no required product purchase or paid ticket is shown in the listing.
  • Purchase required: you may need a qualifying product, minimum spend, receipt or proof of purchase.
  • Paid entry: the promotion appears to require a paid ticket, raffle entry or similar paid participation.
  • App required: entry is completed through the promoter's official app or app-linked flow.
  • Till slip required: keep the receipt because it may be needed for entry validation or prize claims.
  • Account required: you may need a promoter, retailer, loyalty or platform account before entry.

For a fuller breakdown, read the competition entry cost labels guide.

How Freehub verifies competitions

Freehub checks that a listing has enough source information to publish, including a promoter source, closing date, prize cue and entry method where available. Listings that are unclear can be held for verification instead of appearing on public hub pages.

Verification does not mean Freehub runs the competition or guarantees the prize. The official promoter remains responsible for entries, winner selection and prize fulfilment. Read more about how Freehub checks listings.

Competitions ending soon FAQ

Are these competitions free to enter?

Not always. Some competitions are free entry, while others require a purchase, paid entry, an account, an app or a till slip. Check the cost label on Freehub and confirm the full terms on the official promoter page.

How often is this page updated?

Freehub updates competition listings regularly and regenerates this page during site updates. The updated date near the top shows when the page was last built.

Does Freehub run these competitions?

No. Freehub is a competition discovery site. The promoter runs the competition, accepts entries, chooses winners and handles prize fulfilment.

What should I check before entering?

Check the closing date and time, eligibility rules, entry cost, purchase or till slip requirements, official terms, privacy requirements and the promoter's real entry link.

What happens when a competition expires?

Expired competitions are removed from live competition hub pages and should no longer appear as current opportunities. Maintenance may archive or mark listings as closed depending on the data state.
