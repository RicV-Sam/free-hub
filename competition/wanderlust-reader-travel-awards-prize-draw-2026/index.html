Quick answer

The Wanderlust Reader Travel Awards 2026 prize draw is a free international travel-prize draw with ten winners. Enter through the official online awards voting route, follow Wanderlust on Instagram or Facebook, and review the prize-specific travel restrictions before 27 October 2026.

Check the Wanderlust Reader Travel Awards 2026 prize draw: vote online for free, follow Wanderlust on social media and enter before 27 October 2026.

About the brand and this competition

Wanderlust Travel Media publishes the official Reader Travel Awards prize draw terms and names travel prize providers including G Adventures, HX Expeditions, Club Med, YellowWood Adventures, Cox & Kings, Qatar Airways, Abercrombie & Kent, Tropical Sky, Explore Worldwide and New Scientist Discovery Tours.

The official terms list ten travel prizes with a combined approximate value of GBP 76,219, including Peru, Norway, Seychelles, Patagonia, India, Qatar Airways, Kenya, Sri Lanka, Morocco and Northern Spain travel prizes.
