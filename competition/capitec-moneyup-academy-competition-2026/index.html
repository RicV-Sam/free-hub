How to Enter

  1. Use or create a verified MoneyUp Academy account with a registered RSA mobile number.
  2. Earn 4,000 weekly points by completing MoneyUp Academy learning, recap, or quiz activities.
  3. Reach the weekly points target between Monday 00:00 and Sunday 23:59 for one weekly draw entry.
  4. Check your MoneyUp Academy profile and SMS notifications if a Capitec Flexi Voucher is awarded.
