Quick answer

The SPAR Clover Rewards Voucher Competition is a purchase-required voucher promotion. Buy a qualifying Clover product at participating SPAR stores, swipe SPAR Rewards, and qualify before 31 July 2026.

Check the SPAR Clover Rewards competition: buy a qualifying Clover product, swipe SPAR Rewards, and stand a chance to win one of 100 R5,000 SPAR vouchers.

About the brand and this competition

SPAR Group Limited is the promoter and Clover S.A. is the co-promoter on the official SPAR competition page.

The competition offers 100 SPAR gift cards worth R5,000 each, making it a high-value grocery voucher promotion.

Entry cost and eligibility notes

Entry cost note: This is marked as purchase-required because entrants must buy a qualifying Clover product and swipe a SPAR Rewards card.

Eligibility note: A valid SPAR Rewards card and qualifying Clover purchase are required. Users should check the official SPAR terms for full exclusions and verification rules.

How to Enter

  1. Buy any 1 qualifying Clover participating product at a participating SUPERSPAR, SPAR or KWIKSPAR store during the competition period.
  2. Swipe a valid SPAR Rewards card at the point of sale.
  3. Keep the till slip that confirms the competition entry.
  4. Qualify before the competition closes on 31 July 2026.
