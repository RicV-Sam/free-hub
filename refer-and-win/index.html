Prize

R250 airtime

The first campaign prize is fulfilled as an airtime top-up or airtime voucher to a supported South African mobile number.

A WhatsApp number or email lets Freehub contact you about your quick referral link. A supported South African mobile number may still be needed before airtime can be fulfilled if you are selected after review.

Approved referrals

What may count

  • The referred person is new to Freehub Club.
  • The referred person arrives through a valid referral link or referral code.
  • The referred person joins the Club, follows the WhatsApp channel, or creates a quick referral lead that can be reviewed.
  • The referral is not a self-referral.
  • The referral is not duplicate, fake, automated or suspicious.
  • The referral is approved through manual review.

What does not count

Rejected referral examples

  • Self-referrals.
  • Duplicate accounts.
  • Fake or automated registrations.
  • Incomplete or suspicious profiles.
  • Referrals created by misleading users.
  • Referrals that breach the rules or cannot be verified.

Fairness and review

Manual review comes first

Referrals are reviewed before they count. Suspicious activity may be rejected, and the highest count is not final until review is complete.

Freehub may delay, reject or review referrals if there are technical, fraud, abuse or compliance concerns. Public winner announcements will only happen after confirmation.

No public leaderboard

Review stays private

Freehub does not publish emails, mobile numbers, Firebase UIDs or private referral records. Admin review and provisional counts stay inside the private referral admin area.

Winner confirmation happens only after Freehub checks referral quality, participant readiness and rule compliance.

Privacy

Personal details stay private

Freehub uses account and referral information to manage Freehub Club, referral tracking, campaign administration, fraud prevention, and prize fulfilment where applicable.

Marketing messages are optional and require separate consent. Personal information is not shown publicly, and users can unsubscribe from marketing messages later if they opted in.

FAQ

Refer & Win questions

Is Refer & Win live now?

Yes. The first Freehub Refer & Win campaign runs from 18 June 2026 to 31 July 2026, ending at 23:59 SAST on 31 July 2026. It is free to enter, no purchase is required, and referrals only count after manual review.

How do I join Freehub Club?

Join through the Freehub Club page using Google sign-in. Club membership is free, and the quick referral link on this page is available if you want to share before creating a full account.

How do I get a referral link?

Use the quick referral form on this page with a WhatsApp number or email, or sign into Freehub Club and use the referral link in your Club dashboard.

What is the first campaign prize?

The first campaign prize is R250 airtime, fulfilled as an airtime top-up or airtime voucher to a supported South African mobile number.

When does the next campaign run?

The next campaign is planned to run from 1 August 2026 to 31 August 2026, ending at 23:59 SAST on 31 August 2026.

What counts as an approved referral?

Only referrals that pass manual review may count. A click or sign-in alone does not make a referral approved.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals do not count and may be rejected during manual review.

Will there be a public leaderboard?

No. Freehub does not show a public leaderboard for this campaign. Admin review happens privately.

Does Freehub show my personal details publicly?

No. Public referral surfaces must not show emails, phone numbers, Firebase UIDs or private account details.

Do I have to accept marketing messages?

No. Marketing consent is optional and separate from Freehub Club and the Refer & Win campaign.

Where can I read the rules?

Read the campaign rules at /refer-and-win/terms/ before sharing your referral link.
