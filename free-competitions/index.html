How to compare free competitions

What free to enter means on Freehub

Freehub treats a free competition as a listing where the available source information shows no required product purchase, paid ticket, minimum spend, till slip, subscription billing, SMS or USSD cost to enter.

If the cost route is unclear, the listing should not appear on this page. Browse the homepage for broader free giveaways South Africa intent when you want to compare free-entry, purchase-required and account-linked promotions together.

Free entry vs purchase required

A purchase-required competition can still be legitimate, but it belongs on a different page because users must buy something, keep proof of purchase, swipe a loyalty card or meet a spend threshold. Free-entry listings should not require those actions.

Compare purchase mechanics separately on purchase required competitions, scan urgent deadlines on competitions ending soon, or return to all giveaways and competitions when you are not filtering by entry cost.

Common free-entry methods

  • Online forms on the promoter's official website.
  • WhatsApp prompts or social media actions where the official source confirms the route.
  • Radio entries, surveys, quote requests or skill entries that do not require a purchase.
  • App or account forms only where the entry is clearly free and not materially conditional or unclear.

What to check before entering

  • Confirm the promoter, prize, closing date, winner contact method and eligibility rules.
  • Check whether standard data, platform access, age, region, account or privacy terms apply.
  • Use the official promoter link rather than copied forms, comment threads or unofficial messages.
  • Read the official terms even when the listing is marked free entry.

Avoid unofficial winner fees

A free competition should not require unofficial release fees, banking passwords, card PINs or remote access apps to claim a prize. Verify any winner message through the promoter's official website or support channel before responding.

Freehub does not collect entries, choose winners or manage prize fulfilment. You can also browse related prize categories such as cash, vouchers, tech and holidays.

Free competitions FAQ

Are these competitions really free to enter?

These listings pass Freehub's strict free-entry filter, which excludes purchase-required, paid-entry, paid-ticket, till-slip, SMS, USSD and unclear-cost competitions. Always confirm the latest terms on the official promoter page.

What does no purchase required mean?

No purchase required means the available source information does not show a required product purchase, minimum spend, receipt, invoice, loyalty-card purchase or paid ticket before entry.

Can a free competition still require a WhatsApp message or online form?

Yes. A competition can be free to enter and still use an official online form, WhatsApp prompt, social media action, radio entry, survey or similar no-purchase route.

Does Freehub run these competitions?

No. Freehub is a competition discovery site. The promoter runs the competition, accepts entries, chooses winners and handles prize fulfilment. Use the official promoter link on each listing to enter.

What should I check before entering a free competition?

Check the promoter name, closing date, prize, entry method, eligibility rules, privacy terms, winner contact process and whether any account, app, data or platform requirements apply.
